# Audio FX — Tone (Non-Time-Based)

Audio effects that shape the sound without adding time-based effects like reverb or delay. These are about character, color, dynamics, and filtering.

## Sugar Bytes

### WOW2
Vowel filter / modulation filter with four modulation sources (envelope follower, LFO, step sequencer, wobble). Creates wah, vowel, and filter sweep effects.
- **Pedalboard**: 86 params (cutoff, resonance, overdrive, mix_dry_wet, vowel_a/b...)
- **Presets**: ~/Documents/Sugar Bytes/WOW2/Presets/ (.wow files)
- **Best for**: Funky filter sweeps on guitar, vowel effects, auto-wah, folktronica texture

### Artillery2
Massive multi-effect processor with 1025 parameters. Combines multiple effect types in a modular routing system.
- **Pedalboard**: 1025 params
- **Presets**: ~/Documents/Sugar Bytes/Artillery2/Presets/ + FXPresets/
- **Best for**: Complex effect chains, sound design, experimental processing

### Turnado
Real-time multi-effect with 8 effect slots controlled by 8 "dictator" knobs. Map any parameter to any knob for performative effect control.
- **Pedalboard**: 253 params (control_1 through control_8, dictator, dry_wet...)
- **Presets**: ~/Documents/Sugar Bytes/Turnado/
  - Dictator Presets/
  - Effect Presets/
  - Global Presets/
  - Midi Maps/
- **Best for**: Live performance effects, dramatic transitions, risers/drops

### Egoist
Slicer and beat mangler. Slices audio into segments and rearranges, filters, and processes them. Also has a bass synth and beat engine.
- **Pedalboard**: 61 params (master_level, slicer_mute/level, bass_mute/level, beat_mute/level...)
- **Presets**: ~/Documents/Sugar Bytes/Egoist/Presets/ + Bass Presets/
- **Best for**: Glitch effects, beat slicing, remixing, folktronica rhythm

### Looperator
Loop-based effect sequencer. Applies effects to audio in a step-sequenced pattern.
- **Pedalboard**: 116 params (bypass, mix, mainrandom, slicerandom...)
- **Presets**: ~/Documents/Sugar Bytes/Looperator/Presets/
- **Best for**: Rhythmic effects, loop mangling, EDM builds

### Effectrix
Step-sequenced multi-effect. 14 different effects sequenced across a grid pattern. Each step can have different effects.
- **Pedalboard**: 118 params (patternkeys, masterlevel, loop/filter/delay params...)
- **Presets**: ~/Documents/Sugar Bytes/Effectrix/Presets/
- **Sub-presets**: Chorus/, Crush/, Delay/, Filter/, Loop/, Phaser/, Reverb/, Reverse/
- **Best for**: Glitch, stutter, rhythmic effects
- **Note**: Effectrix contains some time-based effects (delay, reverb) but its primary purpose is as a tone/character effect sequencer

## Other Tone FX

### Decapitator (Soundtoys)
Five different analog saturation/distortion models. From subtle warmth to heavy destruction.
- **Best for**: Warming up acoustic guitar recordings, adding grit to electric guitar, tape saturation

### bx_greenscreamer (Plugin Alliance)
Tube Screamer overdrive emulation.
- **Pedalboard**: 5 params (bypass, overdrive, tone, level, setting)
- **Best for**: Guitar overdrive, pushing amps, blues/rock tone

### soothe2 (oeksound)
Dynamic resonance suppressor. Automatically finds and reduces harsh resonances.
- **Pedalboard**: 56 params (depth, sharpness, selectivity, attack, release...)
- **Best for**: Taming harsh acoustic guitar, fixing room resonances, de-essing

### Oxford Inflator (Sonnox)
Loudness enhancer using harmonic processing. Makes things sound louder without just turning up the volume.
- **Best for**: Master bus glue, loudness without limiting, mix punch

### DynOne3 (Leapwing)
Multiband dynamics processor. Splits audio into frequency bands with independent compression.
- **Pedalboard**: 114 params (in_gain, out_gain, per-band threshold/ratio/attack/release...)
- **Best for**: Multiband compression, mix bus dynamics, mastering dynamics

### Surge XT Effects (Surge Synth Team)
Open-source effect suite from the Surge synthesizer. Various effect types.
- **Standalone**: Yes
- **Pedalboard**: 13 params
- **Best for**: Free, high-quality effects
